Reports compating scala collections of different categories.

Scala collections cannot be equal if they are of different categories (Set, Seq, Map or Iterator). Comparing them with arrays is also pointless. Inspection suggests to convert one of the collection to make them of same kind.

Example:


seq == iterator
seq != array

After the quick-fix is applied:


seq.toIterator == iterator or seq == iterator.toSeq
seq != array.toSeq
