Reports array.size and string.size calls.

While such calls are legitimate, they require an additional implicit conversion to SeqLike to be made. A common use case would be calling length on arrays and strings may provide significant advantages.

Example:


Array(1, 2, 3, 4).size
"this is a string".size

After the quick-fix is applied:


Array(1, 2, 3, 4).length
"this is a string".length
