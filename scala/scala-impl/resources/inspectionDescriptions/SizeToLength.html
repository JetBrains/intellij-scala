Reports array.size and string.size calls.

While such calls are legitimate, they require an additional implicit conversion to SeqLike. Calling length on arrays and strings may provide significant advantages.

Example:


  Array(1, 2, 3, 4).size
  "this is a string".size

After the quick-fix is applied:


  Array(1, 2, 3, 4).length
  "this is a string".length
